## What is Cor?

Cor helps your users learn your product. You add a small snippet to your app, and **Obi** — the Cor agent — appears as a widget. Obi walks users through onboarding plans, answers questions, and runs guided sessions.

Cor has three parts:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Obi SDK" icon="code" href="/sdk/installation">
    A JavaScript snippet that puts the Obi widget in your app and tells it who the user is.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Session Reporting API" icon="chart-line" href="/api-reference/introduction">
    Pull session metadata, summaries, and full transcripts for reporting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="User Context API" icon="user" href="/api-reference/introduction">
    Push extra context about a user so Obi can personalise their sessions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How the pieces fit together

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the SDK">
    Add the Obi snippet to your app. The widget loads on every page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify the user">
    Tell Obi who the signed-in user is. This powers targeting, personalisation, and reporting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run sessions">
    Obi guides users through onboarding plans — automatically or when you start one in code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Report and enrich">
    Use the APIs to pull transcripts and summaries, and to push extra context back to Obi.
  </Step>
</Steps>
